## Understanding Intelligent Test Case Selection

Intelligent test case selection is the process of selecting the most relevant and effective test cases from a test suite to validate the software's functionality. It leverages machine learning algorithms and predictive analytics to determine which test cases are most likely to identify defects in the software, thereby maximizing testing efficiency.

### The Need for Intelligent Test Case Selection

According to a report by Statista, the cost of software failure is estimated to reach $1.1 trillion globally by 2021. With software becoming increasingly complex, the cost and time involved in exhaustive testing is skyrocketing. Intelligent test case selection targets this problem by reducing the number of test cases required, thereby saving time and resources.
