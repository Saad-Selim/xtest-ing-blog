## What is Production Sanity Testing?

Sanity testing, also known as smoke testing, is a type of software testing that comprises a non-exhaustive set of tests that validate the basic functionalities of a system after minor changes or repairs. The purpose of sanity testing is to ascertain that the changes or fixes have not adversely affected the core functionalities of the system.

### Why is Sanity Testing Crucial?

In today’s competitive software market, frequent updates are common to enhance the user experience or fix bugs. However, these updates can sometimes inadvertently introduce new issues. This is where sanity testing comes in. It helps to quickly identify any detrimental impacts of changes or updates on the application’s performance, saving time and resources.

### Real-World Applications and Benefits of Sanity Testing

Sanity testing is used across various industries. For example, in the gaming industry, sanity tests are conducted after every update to ensure the game’s core functionalities are not affected. Similarly, in the eCommerce sector, sanity tests are performed during peak sales periods to ensure a seamless shopping experience.

Some of the key benefits of sanity testing include:

*   Reduced risk of bugs going into production
*   Quick identification of issues after changes or updates
*   Increased user satisfaction due to improved software quality
