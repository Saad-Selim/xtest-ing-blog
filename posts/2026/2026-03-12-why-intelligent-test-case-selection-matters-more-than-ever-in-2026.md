## What is Intelligent Test Case Selection?

Intelligent Test Case Selection is a modern-day testing methodology that leverages algorithms and machine learning to select the most relevant and crucial test cases from a large suite. By doing so, it significantly reduces the time and resources required for exhaustive testing, while ensuring a high level of software quality.

### How Does ITCS Work in Xtest?

In Xtest, ITCS is implemented through a robust analysis of historical test data. The platform's algorithms evaluate the criticality, relevance, and potential impact of each test case based on various factors such as code complexity, defect history, and change frequency. This data-driven approach ensures that only the most valuable test cases are executed, leading to optimal resource utilization and enhanced test coverage.

### 1\. Enhanced Test Efficiency

By focusing on the most relevant test cases, ITCS minimizes the time and effort spent on unnecessary tests. This leads to faster testing cycles and quicker time to market.

### 2\. Improved Test Coverage

With ITCS, resources are utilized more effectively as teams no longer need to run exhaustive test suites. Instead, they can concentrate their efforts on the most impactful test cases.
