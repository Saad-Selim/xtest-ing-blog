## What is Visual Regression Testing?

Visual Regression Testing is a quality assurance process that involves comparing an application's visual elements before and after modifications. Its primary purpose is to identify unintended visual changes and inconsistencies. This type of testing is critical in today's digital landscape, where even a minor visual glitch can negatively impact user experience and brand reputation.

## Why is Visual Regression Testing Important?

According to a report by the World Quality Report 2020-21, improving the end-user experience is the top priority for 39% of respondents. With such an emphasis on user experience, ensuring visual consistency becomes paramount. Here are some reasons why Visual Regression Testing is indispensable:

*   It helps in identifying visual bugs that might be missed during functional testing.
*   It helps in ensuring consistency across different devices and browsers.
*   It saves time by automating the process of checking visual elements.
